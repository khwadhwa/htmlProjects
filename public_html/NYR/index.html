One of the NHL's original six teams, The New York Rangers are New York's oldest hockey team. The Rangers have lifted the Stanley Cup in 1928, 1933, 1940, and 1994.


They compete in the National Hockey League as a member of the Metropolitan Division in the Eastern Conference.
